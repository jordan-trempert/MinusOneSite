How to play

Everything you need to know.

Goal

Reduce opponents to -1 HP to eliminate them. Survive until you’re the last player standing.

On your turn

  1. Play a legal card (match color or number, or play a special).
  2. If you can’t play, you must draw until you can (depending on lobby rules).
  3. Special cards change turn order and pacing.

Damage & gold

Number cards deal damage (and generate gold). Gold fuels Power cards and the Shop.

Card types

Play smart, break tempo.

Number cards

  • Legal play: match color or match number.
  • Damage: based on value (plus augment bonuses).
  • Gold: earn gold when you play.

Special cards

  • Skip: deny the next turn.
  • Reverse: flip the direction of play.
  • Wild: always playable, choose the next color.

Power cards

  • Purchased/drawn with gold.
  • Examples: Shield, Heals, Double Damage, Steal gold.
  • Some are negative and trigger immediately on draw.

Between rounds: Shop + Deck rigging

This is where runs are made.

Augments

Buy upgrades that permanently boost your run: damage bonuses, survivability, economy, power-card capacity, and more.

Deck fixes

Rig your deck: inject colors, remove junk, and shape your odds. Small changes add up over multiple rounds.

Timer

Shop time is limited (and configurable in lobbies). Decide fast: stabilize, scale, or gamble on a swing.

Advanced tips

Win more fights.

Play to the next seat

You always hit the next player in the current direction. Use Reverse to change who you’re pressuring.

Protect your tempo

Skips and Wilds are tempo tools. Hold them to deny lethal turns or set up a safe power-card window.

Spend gold with intent

Gold can be damage, safety, or scaling. Don’t buy everything—buy the thing that wins the next two turns.
